My name is Rachel McClain Robinson and I look forward to supporting you!

I aid clients to balance out overwhelming emotions and thoughts through psychotherapy. I draw from several theoretical orientations based on client needs, such as:

Motivational Interviewing

  • Core Mindfulness
  • Distress Tolerance
  • Emotional Regulation
  • Interpersonal Effectiveness

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

The main theory behind Cognitive Behavioral Therapy is that your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors all impact each other.

Solution-Focused Therapy

Solution-focused therapy is a future-focused, goal-directed approach to therapy that highlights the importance of searching for solutions.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy

DBT focuses on skills training and includes mindfulness, emotion regulation, interpersonal effectiveness and distress tolerance.
